--- layout: page title: About slug: about order: 1 --- ![cover](https://github.com/jgmaz/jgmaz.github.io/blob/master/images/cover.jpg) I am a PhD candidate in the lab of Matthijs van der Meer in the Psychological and Brain Sciences department at Dartmouth College. I am broadly interested in the neural dynamics that support decision-making. My current focus in the van der Meer lab is understanding how coordinated activity within the nucleus accumbens contributes to the efficient pursuit of goals.
